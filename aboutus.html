About Us

Dong Hae Korean Grill & Sushi is a family owned restaurant that offers authentic Korean cuisine, fresh sushi, and delicious dining or takeout in the downtown Minneapolis, Minnesota. We pride ourselves in producing delicious dishes and providing the best customer service.

It is a cornerstone in the Minneapolis community that has been recognized for its outstanding sushi cuisine, excellent service, and friendly staff. Our restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high-quality fresh ingredients. Please enjoy your visit. We hope that our family’s establishment continues to serve special individuals like you in our community.

Head Chef
